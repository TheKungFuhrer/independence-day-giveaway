6. Referral / Dual-Winner Mechanic (Optional)

Referring others is completely optional and is NOT required to enter or to win. Every valid entry has the same chance in the Grand Prize drawing whether or not the entrant refers anyone.

Upon submitting a valid online entry, each entrant is issued a unique referral link. If an entrant chooses to share that link and a person who enters through it is selected as the Grand Prize winner in the random drawing, then both the referred person (the drawn winner) and the entrant who referred them (the referrer) will each receive the Grand Prize, subject to all eligibility and redemption conditions in these Official Rules.

Conditions and limits:

  • To receive the Grand Prize as a referrer under this mechanic, the referrer must themselves be an eligible Grand Prize entrant (i.e., a U.S. resident, 18+, who owns or operates an active U.S. event venue) and must satisfy all winner conditions in Sections 10–11. A referrer who is not Grand-Prize-eligible may still compete for the $500 Cash Referral Prize (Section 7) but cannot receive a Grand Prize.
  • The dual-winner award applies only to the direct referrer of the drawn winner (one level only); chains of referrals do not cascade.
  • Self-referral, referral of fictitious persons, referral of persons who do not independently qualify, and any automated, bulk, or fraudulent referral activity are prohibited and will void the referral and may void all of the offending entrant's entries.
  • Capacity cap. In the event the dual-winner mechanic would result in more Grand Prize awards than the Sponsor can reasonably fulfill, the Sponsor reserves the right to limit the total number of Grand Prizes awarded and/or to substitute a prize of equal or greater value, in its sole discretion, consistent with these Official Rules.

7. $500 Cash Referral Prize (Skill / Effort-Based)

Separate and apart from the random-drawing Grand Prize, the Sponsor will award one (1) $500 USD Cash Referral Prize to the entrant who generates the most qualified referrals during the Entry Period. This is an effort-based award, not a game of chance — it is determined by measurable performance, not by random selection.

Open to all entrants. Any valid entrant may compete for the $500 Cash Referral Prize, whether or not they own or operate a venue. (This gives non-venue-owners a way to participate and win.)

What counts as a “qualified referral.” A “qualified referral” is a distinct individual who: (a) enters the Sweepstakes through the competing entrant's unique referral link (or referral code), (b) completes the online entry form in full, and (c) independently meets the Grand Prize eligibility criteria in Section 3 (i.e., is a U.S. resident, 18+, who owns or operates an active U.S. event venue) as verified by the Sponsor. Referrals that are incomplete, duplicative, self-referrals, fraudulent, bot-generated, or that do not independently qualify will not be counted.

Tally method. The Sponsor will tally each competing entrant's qualified referrals based on its referral-tracking system as of the close of the Entry Period (July 6, 2026 at 12:00 PM ET). The Sponsor's records are final and determinative.

Tie-breaker. In the event of a tie for the most qualified referrals, the prize will be awarded to the tied entrant whose qualifying referrals reached the qualifying threshold earliest (i.e., the entrant who recorded their highest qualified-referral count at the earliest timestamp). If a tie still remains, the Sponsor will award the prize to the tied entrant whose own entry was received earliest. The Sponsor's determination is final.

The $500 Cash Referral Prize will be paid by Stripe (ACH or card payout) to the verified winner within thirty (30) days after winner verification and receipt of any required tax/identity documentation (see Sections 8 and 11).
